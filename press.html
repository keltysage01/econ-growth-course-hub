Long description (150 words)

eCon Growth builds AI Operating Systems for serious operators — the operations layer behind serious businesses. Co-founded in 2025 by Kristopher Cravens (Founder & CEO) and Watson Wheeler (Co-Founder & COO), the company works across three lines: Operations (custom AI Operating Systems that run the day-to-day of a business), Marketing, and Financial Planning. The thesis is simple — serious businesses lose time and money to work that should run on a system, not on one person's memory.

Command HVAC is the company's flagship product: the AI Operating System for HVAC contractors, proven in production. It includes Roger, the 24/7 AI voice agent that answers, qualifies, and books every inbound call in under three seconds. Both run on Anthropic's Claude. Command HVAC replaces owner-dependent operations with systematic AI dispatch, real-time visibility, coaching analytics, and weekly business intelligence — the model eCon Growth applies across verticals.

Leadership

Kristopher Cravens — Founder & CEO

Kristopher Cravens spent ten years inside HVAC operations before founding eCon Growth. Every role, every truck, every shift — from intake to dispatch to field service to ownership. That operator's eye is the foundation of how eCon Growth builds: from the inside out, for the people actually running the business. He led the build of Command HVAC, the company's flagship product.

Watson Wheeler — Co-Founder & COO

Watson Wheeler is Co-Founder and COO of eCon Growth, focused on scaling, operations, and exit strategy. He builds the operational infrastructure — documented workflows, structured systems, clean reporting — that turns a business into a sellable asset instead of a treadmill.
